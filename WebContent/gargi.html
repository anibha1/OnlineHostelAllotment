    Gargi Hostel


    Highlights

    1. Gargi hostel is named after the great Indian philosopher, Gargi Vachaknavi. She is widely known as 'Brahmavadini'.
    2. The warden of Gargi hostel is Prof. Bindeshwara Singh. He is one of the esteemed faculty of KNIT.
    3. Gargi hostel is meant for the allotement of second year students. .
    4. The hostel is divided in three wings - two single seater wings and one three seater wing.
    5. The hostel has a capacity to accomodate almost 280 students, the highest among all the hostels.
    6. It has its own mess facility. It has a basketball court and two spacious common halls.
    7. It is the biggest hostel of KNIT in terms of space and accomodation.
