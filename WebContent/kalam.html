    Kalam Hostel


    Highlights

    1. Kalam hostel is named after the great Indian scientist and President,Dr. A.P.J.Abdul Kalam. UPTU is named AKTU after him.
    2. The warden of Kalam hostel is Prof. Amit Medhavi. He also has the charge of the New VS hostel.
    3. Kalam hostel is meant for the allotement of first year students.
    4. The hostel is divided in three wings - the ground wing with G1 to G8, the first floor with F1 to F8 and the S wing with S1 to S8.
    5. The hostel has a total of 39 rooms so that it could accomodate more than 150 students.
    6. It has a common hall,library and reading room,warden room, guest room and a recreation rooom.
    7. It is the newest hostel of KNIT. Earlier the 'Blood Donation Camp' was organised in this hostel only.
